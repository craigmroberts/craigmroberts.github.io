Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Most custom Shopify builds take 6-10 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on complexity. Simple theme customizations can be completed in 2-3 weeks.

Do you work with Shopify Plus only?

I work with both standard Shopify and Shopify Plus. While I specialize in Plus implementations for enterprise clients, I'm equally comfortable working with smaller brands on standard plans.

What's your pricing structure?

Project pricing varies based on scope, typically ranging from ¬£5,000 for theme customizations to ¬£30,000+ for full custom builds. I also offer hourly consulting at ¬£150/hr and monthly retainers for ongoing support.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. I also offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing maintenance, updates, and optimization.

Can you help migrate from another platform to Shopify?

Absolutely. I've successfully migrated stores from WooCommerce, Magento, and custom platforms to Shopify while preserving SEO rankings and customer data.
